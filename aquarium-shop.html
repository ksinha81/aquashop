📖 Step-by-Step Guide

This guide explains how shop owners and customers can use AquaShop. Share this link with anyone!

👨‍💼 FOR SHOP OWNERS
1

Open the Admin Panel

Click the ⚙️ Admin tab at the top. Enter your username and password to log in.

💡 Default login: Username = admin   Password = aquashop123 — Change this in the config!
2

Add Your Fish

Fill in the Fish Name, Type, Emoji, Price (₹), Quantity and a short description. Click 💾 Save Fish. The fish instantly appears in the customer catalog!

💡 To update stock later, click ✏️ Edit next to any fish and change the quantity.
3

Set Up Your UPI QR Code

In the 💳 UPI / Payment Settings box, enter your UPI ID (e.g. yourshop@paytm), your shop name, and paste the URL of your UPI QR code image. Customers will see this at checkout.

💡 To get a QR image URL: Open Google Pay / PhonePe → Your QR → Screenshot → Upload to imgbb.com → Copy the direct image link.
4

Share the Website Link with Customers

Host this file for free on Netlify: Go to app.netlify.com/drop, drag and drop this HTML file, and get a free link like https://amazing-fish-123.netlify.app. Share it on WhatsApp, Instagram, or print it as a QR!

💡 It's completely free and takes under 1 minute to publish!
5

Monitor Your Inventory

The Admin Panel shows you a live dashboard: total fish types, total stock, how many are low stock, and how many are out of stock. When a customer places an order, stock is automatically reduced.

🛍️ FOR CUSTOMERS
1

Browse Fish

Use the 🔍 search bar to find any fish by name, or use the dropdown to filter by type (Freshwater, Saltwater, Tropical, Coldwater). Each card shows the price, availability, and stock count.

2

Add to Cart & Review

Click 🛒 Add to Cart on any available fish. Go to the 🛒 Cart tab to review your items, adjust quantities, or remove anything. The total with tax is shown at the bottom.

3

Checkout in 3 Simple Steps

Step 1 – Review your order items and total.
Step 2 – Address – Enter your name, phone, and delivery address.
Step 3 – Pay – Choose UPI (scan the QR), Cash, or Card. For UPI, scan the QR code shown on screen and enter your UTR/transaction number to confirm.

💡 You'll get an Order Number at the end — save it as proof of purchase!
